Help us to change lives!

There is very little in the way of relief agencis in our area and so when
disaster strikes people turn to us for help. In the last few years, we have seen horrible
flooding in our district which claimed many lives. Our team of volunteers assisted not just in providing
food, accommodation and rescue but also post-disaster support through means of construction throught means of contruction/repair
, removal of fallen trees and disposal of dead animals/livestock to prevent the spread of disease.
