Sweing Training Center

The center provides free training to local village women and empowers themn to be able
to generate a regular and secure income for their households. Classes run by a local tailor
operate daily both as a means of instruction and also income generation for the students as
thy stitch simple items for shops from the local market;
pillowcases, bed-sheets, bags, sacks and curtains.just to name a few!.
